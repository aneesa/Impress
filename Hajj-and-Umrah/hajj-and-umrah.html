Hajj and Umrah
Aneesa Awaludin
Hajj

Hajj is the pilgrimage to the Sacred House (Ka'bah) in Makkah during the months of Hajj to perform specific acts of worship according to its prescribed conditions.

Haji ialah mengunjungi Baitullah Al-Haram (Ka'bah) di Makkah pada bulan-bulan Haji untuk mengerjakan ibadat-ibadat tertentu menurut syarat-syaratnya.

Umrah

Umrah is the pilgrimage to the Sacred House (Ka'bah) in Makkah at any time to perform specific acts of worship according to its prescribed conditions.

Umrah ialah mengunjungi Baitullah Al-Haram (Ka'bah) di Makkah pada bila-bila masa untuk mengerjakan ibadat tertentu menurut syarat-syaratnya.

Prohibitions of Ihram
  • 1. Wearing stitched or body-fitting clothing (for men)
  • 2. Covering the head or any part of it (for men)
  • 3. Covering the face (for women)
  • 4. Wearing gloves
  • 5. Applying oil to the head or beard
  • 6. Wearing or smelling perfume, whether on the body or clothing
Prohibitions of Ihram
  • 7. Foreplay (acts leading to intercourse)
  • 8. Engaging in sexual intercourse
  • 9. Getting married, officiating a marriage, or acting as a marriage representative
  • 10. Removing hair or body hair
  • 11. Clipping or cutting the nails
  • 12. Cutting down, uprooting, or damaging trees or plants
  • 13. Hunting or killing land game animals
